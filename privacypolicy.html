Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to Braita. This policy explains how KITAAS SOLUTIONS manages your data. Your privacy and trust are our top priorities.

Important Note: Providing personal information such as your name, age, gender, or district is entirely optional. You can use all features of the app without providing these details.

1. Information Collection and Choice

We believe in giving you full control over your data. You may choose to provide the following information to personalize your profile:

  • Optional Profile Details: First Name, Last Name, Age, Gender, and District. This information is only collected if you choose to update your profile.
  • Automatic Identifiers: We collect your unique Device ID. This is mandatory to sync your quiz progress and daily limits to our database so you don't lose your data.

2. Notification System & Permissions

To help you stay on track with your daily quiz goals, Braita uses a notification system. This requires the following permissions:

  • Post Notifications: To show you daily reminders and quiz updates.
  • Exact Alarms & Reminders: Used to trigger your daily 7 PM reminder and welcome alerts at a precise time, even when the app is in the background.
  • Local Storage: We use local timezone data (Asia/Colombo) to ensure reminders are delivered at the correct time based on your location.

3. Use of Database for Alerts

We utilize Firebase Realtime Database to send you important updates. The app listens to a specific notification node to display real-time messages regarding new quizzes, app updates, or ranking news. This process does not collect personal messages from your device; it only fetches public broadcast data from our server.

4. How We Use Your Data

Information provided is used for:

  • Personalization: To display your chosen name and details on your profile page.
  • Hero Ranking: To show your name (if provided) or "Visitor" on the leaderboard.
  • Syncing: Using your Device ID to ensure your scores and daily 20-quiz progress are saved correctly.
  • Reminders: To notify you at 7 PM daily to complete your 20-quiz goal for the Hero Ranking.

5. Data Control and Deletion

Since profile details are optional, you can change or remove your name, age, or district at any time through the app settings. You can also disable notifications at any time via your device system settings. You have the right to request full deletion of all data linked to your Device ID.

6. Security

We use industry-standard Firebase security protocols and the flutter_local_notifications framework to protect both your optional profile data and your notification preferences.
